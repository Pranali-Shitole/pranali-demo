Inception – A Fan Page

About the Movie

*Inception* is a 2010 science fiction action thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. Known for its mind-bending visuals and complex storyline, the film explores the concept of dreams within dreams and features a stellar cast led by Leonardo DiCaprio.

Main Cast

  • Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb
  • Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur
  • Elliot Page as Ariadne
  • Tom Hardy as Eames
  • Ken Watanabe as Saito
  • Marion Cotillard as Mal
